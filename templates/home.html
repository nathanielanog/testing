FEATURES

Smart projector designed for smart
classroom environments

Enhances classroom learning with seamless connectivity, gesture-based control, and an interactive experience built for modern education.

Seamless connectivity
Enables lecture presentation without plugging device into HDMI, minimizing technical errors and ensuring a smooth experience.
Gesture-based control
Interprets hand gestures as commands, enabling instructors to navigate slides, control media, and adjust settings.
Web interface
Lets users log in and upload files, providing a simple and intuitive way to manage presentations.
